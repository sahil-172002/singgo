About Singgo Ludro

Singgo Ludro has dedicated his career to public service, fighting for the rights and well-being of our community. With over 15 years of experience in public office, he continues to champion education reform, economic development, and environmental protection.

Life & Career

Early Life & Education

Born in 1975 in Central Java, Singgo Ludro grew up in a middle-class family that emphasized the importance of education and public service. He graduated with honors from the University of Indonesia with a degree in Political Science in 1997, followed by a Master's in Public Policy from the National University of Singapore in 2000.

Early Career

Before entering politics, Ludro worked as a community organizer and social activist, focusing on youth education and environmental conservation. His grassroots work in Jakarta's underserved communities laid the foundation for his future political career.

Political Journey

First elected to the Jakarta Regional People's Representative Council in 2009, Ludro quickly established himself as a voice for progressive reform. He has served on various committees, including Education, Environmental Protection, and Economic Development.

Achievements

Throughout his career, Ludro has successfully initiated several key programs, including: - The Green Jakarta Initiative (2015) - Youth Employment Program (2018) - Educational Technology Access Program (2020) - Small Business Development Fund (2022)
